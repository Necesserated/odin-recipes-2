How to Make Roof Pizza

Begin

You are not under obligation to follow this recipe to its core. Feel free to experiment. The ingredients are just there to guide your creativity.

Ingredients

  • Handful of seaweed
  • Ten dirt clumps
  • Leftover dough from Chuck E. Cheese© dumpster
  • Human nail clippings
  • Fresh mud (quantity unspecified)
  • Garden tomatoes
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Lay out dough on flat or incline surface.
  2. Slap some ketchup on it, spread evenly with a brush.
  3. Carefully layer frozen dino nuggets.
  4. Sprinkle a cup of cheese
  5. Rub seaweed in mud for earthy flavor and generously layer.
